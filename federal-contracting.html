Where We Work

Priority Agencies & Contract Vehicles

SLBNow pursues both prime awards and subcontracting opportunities across major IDIQ vehicles at priority agencies. Open to first-tier and lower-tier subcontracting relationships with primes and higher-tier subs, as well as teaming arrangements.

Priority Agency
Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)

VA IT and healthcare program vehicles, including IHT 2.0 (Contract 36C10X25D0019) — a $14B, 10-year SDVOSB IDIQ. SLBNow is positioned for both prime pursuit and subcontracting under SDVOSB primes on healthcare IT and program support task orders.

Priority Agency
Department of Defense (DoD)

DoD program management, IT governance, and compliance support under T4NG2 and other IDIQ vehicles. CyberAB RPO credential adds direct CMMC advisory value to DoD programs and prime contractors seeking compliant partners.

Priority Agency
HHS & CMS

Health and Human Services program support, healthcare IT governance, and Medicaid/Medicare IT compliance (45 CFR §95.626). Clinical credentials on staff are directly applicable to HHS healthcare program task orders.

Priority Agency
GSA / Multiple Award Schedules

Program support and IT management services available under GSA schedule vehicles. SLBNow is pursuing GSA schedule access as a near-term vehicle goal. Direct engagement with primes and agencies currently available pending schedule award.
