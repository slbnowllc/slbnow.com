The Problem Most Organizations Miss

Why General IT Support Falls Short

Commercial MSPs are built for commercial environments with commercial documentation standards. Organizations operating under compliance obligations — CMMC, DFARS, HIPAA, or internal audit requirements — need IT governance that produces evidence, not just results.

Documentation Gaps

Auditors and assessors evaluate how controls operate in practice — not just whether a policy exists. Most general IT providers rarely produce the structured, traceable evidence that regulated environments require.

Boundary Risk

For defense contractors: if your IT provider has administrative access to systems that touch FCI or CUI, they are inside your CMMC assessment boundary. Their practices, documentation, and change management become your compliance exposure.

Reactive vs. Governed

General IT support is reactive. Governed environments require structured change control, documented configurations, and traceable actions — every time. A ticket-driven model cannot produce audit-ready evidence when it matters.
